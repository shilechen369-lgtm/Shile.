ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Shile.

I am a current student majoring in Digital Art Computing at Goldsmiths University. I am proficient in using TouchDesigner and Arena to create immersive digital art works that integrate audio and visuals.
Driven by my passion for electronic music, I also utilize VCV Rack to produce experimental audio and combine with visual dynamics effect to create real-time interactive experiences.

I hope this personal portfolio can fully present my creative style, allowing viewers to better understand the concepts and meanings behind the works and generate their own interpretations from them.

Thank you for your reading, look forward to your evaluation.
